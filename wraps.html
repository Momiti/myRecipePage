🌮 Wraps

4-6 portions

Difficulty: Very easy

What you'll need

  • 1 package of wraps
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 can of red beans
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Avocado
  • Optional: Falafel

Preparation

Cut tomato, salad and cucumber. Prepare corn and the red beans. Warm the wraps in the oven for a very short time. Cut the avocado, mash it and use it as a base on the wrap. Add pepper if you wish so. Add the cut greens. Fold the wrap and enjoy! Tip: You can vary the ingredients as you like.
