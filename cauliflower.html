🥔 Cauliflower and Potatos

4 portions

Difficulty: Easy

What you'll need

  • 1 cauliflower
  • 300g potatos
  • 1 onion
  • Nutmeg
  • Optional: Tomatos and lamb's lettuce

Preparation

Cut the onion and steam it in a pan with oil. Wash the cauliflower, cut it in small flowers and add them in the pan. Peel and wash the potatos and boild them in salt water. Keep an eye on the cauliflower so that it's not burning in the pan. It might take some time until it's ready. Serve the cauliflower together with potatos and add nutmeg if you like. You can also add cut tomatos and lettuce.
